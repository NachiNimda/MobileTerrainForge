## Brand Tagline Guidelines

### Dos
- Keep it under 15 words (for punchy impact)
- Use active verbs (Generate, Build, Optimize)
- Include concrete numbers when possible (75%, 62%, 33%)
- Be honest â€” no exaggerated claims
- Sound confident, not arrogant

### Don'ts
- Don't use buzzwords (revolutionary, groundbreaking, game-changing)
- Don't make promises you can't keep
- Don't be cute or clever at the expense of clarity
- Don't copy competitors' style
- Don't change taglines frequently
