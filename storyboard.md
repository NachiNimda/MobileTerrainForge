### Visual Style Guide

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a)
- Text: White (#ffffff)
- Accent: Muted gold (#c9a227) — sparing use
- Negative space: 60% of frame minimum

**Typography:**
- Font: Inter or SF Pro (clean, professional)
- Headings: Bold, tracking -2%
- Body: Regular, tracking 0%
- No all-caps (except tool name)

**Motion:**
- No flashy transitions
- Ease-in-out preferred
- Camera movements: Smooth pans, zooms only for emphasis
- No shake, no glitch effects

**Graphics:**
- WFC visualization: Clean grid lines, tiles snap magnetically
- LOD zones: Subtle colored overlays, no harsh edges
- Splatmap: Technical diagram style, minimal
- Benchmark data: Simple bar charts, no animations
